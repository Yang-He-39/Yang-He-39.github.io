Scope

Transfer pricing documentation + method workflows
  • Assist with TPD drafting and structure (facts, functional analysis, method narrative, supporting rationale).
  • Retrieve and cite relevant tax regulation/internal guidance; maintain traceability of sources.
  • Provide method workflow support (e.g., selection logic, comparability considerations, sanity checks).

Workflow outputs

Designed for KNIME / Alteryx integration
  • Structured outputs: templates, tables, checklists, and “method rationale” blocks.
  • Export formats for automation: JSON/CSV + report-ready text blocks.
  • Composable steps: retrieval → draft → review → finalize → export to workflow tools.

Safety & evaluation

Reliability in consulting settings
  • Human-in-the-loop checkpoints for sensitive decisions and final deliverables.
  • Evaluation against reference cases: faithfulness, completeness, and consistency.
  • Guardrails: “show sources”, uncertainty handling, and structured response constraints.
